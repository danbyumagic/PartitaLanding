Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Overview

Partita is a sheet music reader for Android. Partita is designed to run primarily on your device. We do not require user accounts and we do not operate a backend service that stores your personal score library.

Data Stored On Your Device

When you use Partita, the app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • PDF files you import into the app
  • Annotations you create on scores
  • Library metadata such as title, composer, and setlist information
  • Preferences such as display and tool settings
  • Your last-viewed page for each score

Network Activity

Partita does not use analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking SDKs. If and when external music-library integrations are used from within the app, requests are sent directly to those third-party services. Partita does not proxy those requests through its own servers.

What We Do Not Collect

Partita is not designed to collect or sell personal data. We do not collect:

  • Advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Microphone recordings
  • Usage analytics linked to your identity

Permissions

Permission Purpose
Internet Used only for app features that require fetching online resources.
Storage access (system file picker) Lets you import PDF files and export backups you explicitly choose.

Data Deletion

You can delete Partita data at any time by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app from your Android device.

Children's Privacy

Partita is not intended to collect personal information from children or adults. If you believe data was collected in error, contact us and we will investigate.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. Any material changes will be reflected on this page by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/danbyumagic/partita/issues .
