Android PDF Score Viewer

Partita keeps your repertoire ready to perform.

Built for conservatory students and professional musicians, Partita gives Android a serious, distraction-free score experience: fast imports, dark reading mode, and instant resume to your last page.

Why Partita

Clean by design Every element has a purpose. No visual noise, no ad clutter.
Library first Import PDF scores quickly and keep your collection organized.
Performance flow Reopen exactly where you left off with page progress memory.

Current Build Status

Core app architecture is in place with Room, Compose, score import, and viewer progress tracking. Library and full-screen viewer UI are actively being finalized.
